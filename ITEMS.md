## Rarity

| Rarity    | Drop chance | Sell price | Buy price |
|-----------|-------------|------------|-----------|
| Common    | 60%         | 5g         | 15g       |
| Uncommon  | 25%         | 15g        | 45g       |
| Rare      | 12%         | 40g        | 120g      |
| Epic      | 3%          | 120g       | 360g      |
| Legendary | 0.01%*      | 500g       | Not sold  |

> *Legendary items only drop from boss enemies (Dragon, Abyssal Titan). All other kills that roll legendary are demoted to Epic.

## Special Effects Reference

| Effect               | Description |
|----------------------|-------------|
| Lifesteal X%         | Heals the player for X% of damage dealt per hit |
| Poison X%            | Deals X% of weapon DPS as additional damage-over-time |
| Dodge X%             | X% chance to completely avoid an incoming attack |
| Block X%             | X% chance to negate an incoming attack |
| Armor ignore X%      | Reduces effective enemy DEF by X% before damage calculation |
| Spell amp X%         | Increases mage spell damage by X% |
| Crit threshold N+    | Crits on d20 roll of N or higher (rogue default: 17) |
| Doublecast X%        | X% chance to cast a second attack in the same turn (mage) |
| Atk speed +X%        | Reduces time between player attacks by X% |
| Regen on kill X%     | Restores X% of max HP each time an enemy dies |
